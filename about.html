PIPA Technological Capabilities

PIPA specializes in advancing technology through research and development in bioinformatics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. We also offer IT integration, software design, and development services tailored to industry needs. Our enterprise cloud applications and services harness state-of-the-art AI/ML, bioinformatics, and software engineering to empower partners with transformative tools.

These technologies enable seamless process optimization, the integration of siloed workflows, and data-driven decision-making that accelerates scientific discovery and drives product innovation across nutrition, food, ingredients, and health industries. A dedicated team of top-tier AI/ML experts, scientists, and software engineers continually refines the advanced frameworks powering PIPA solutions.

Machine Learning & AI: PIPA’s Edge

PIPA’s Data Science and Machine Learning teams utilize cutting-edge public and proprietary frameworks to enhance and integrate scientific knowledge. Our innovative Knowledge Graphs, advanced search modalities, and intuitive dashboards revolutionize research and discovery processes. These tools empower unparalleled efficiency and precision in Nutrition, Food, and Life Science R&D, significantly reducing costs, resource demands, and risks while accelerating innovation.

Bioinformatics & Chemoinformatics: PIPA’s Expertise

PIPA leverages in-house bioinformatics and chemoinformatics pipelines to integrate public and proprietary data, delivering advanced insights into cohort identification, taxonomic and metabolic pathway abundances, gene expression patterns, differentially abundant features, and bioactivity predictions based on structure and sequence analysis. Our commitment to best practices in data integration and harmonization ensures a comprehensive and accurate representation of biomedical, biochemical, and nutritional entities across all our solutions.
